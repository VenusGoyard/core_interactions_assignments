How To Make Uyghur Polo (داستىخان شاھى- پولو)

Introduction

Polo is a traditional Uyghur rice dish, it’s one of the most famous halal Muslim street foods in Central Asia, which we consider to be “King of the Uyghur Table.”

Ingredients

  • lamb 300g
  • canola oil 160ml
  • red onion 1x
  • carrots 8x
  • raisins 60g
  • rice 500g
  • cold water 1.4l
  • seasonings: salt, cumin, and sugar to taste

Steps

  1. Cut the lamb roughly in 5-6 big sections. Cut the onion in half and slice them into half rings. Julienne the carrots.
  2. Rinse the rice to remove the surface starch that otherwise causes the rice to clump together. Let the rice soak in clear water for a bit. Set aside.
  3. Add the oil to the pan over high heat, when the oil is hot, add the meat and brown them up on all sides.
  4. Add in the onions, cumin, salt, carrots, and sugar. Mix all the ingredients carefully and gently. Let it cook (about 8-10 min.)
  5. Dry the rice. Bring the rice into the pan and spread the rice evenly out on top of the rest of the ingredients.
  6. Cover and keep it at a low simmer for about 35 min. Then, mix all the ingredients gently from the bottom of the pan.
